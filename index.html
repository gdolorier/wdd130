Spain

Spain is located in Europe, it is a Mediterranean country and much of the lifestyle of its inhabitants is based on their relationship with this sea. We have a varied gastronomy, often considered among the best and healthiest in the world. In addition, we have many tourist and cultural places where we can see the heritage of our ancestors.
